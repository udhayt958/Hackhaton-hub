About NCT Hackathon

The Niagara College Toronto Hackathon is an annual event that brings together students, developers, designers, and innovators from across the region to collaborate, create, and compete.

Our mission is to foster innovation, encourage collaboration, and provide a platform for participants to showcase their technical skills and creative problem-solving abilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the NCT Hackathon

What is NCT Hackathon 2025?

NCT Hackathon 2025 is a coding and innovation event where students collaborate to build solutions for real-world problems within a limited time. The hackathon will run for 48 hours, during which teams will conceptualize, design, and build their projects.

Who can participate in NCT Hackathon?

The hackathon is open to students from all backgrounds, whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer. We welcome participants with various skills including programming, design, business, and presentation.

Where will NCT Hackathon be held?

The event will take place at Niagara College Toronto campus, with online participation options available. The address is 740 Bathurst Street, Toronto, ON, M5S 2R6, Canada.

How do I register for NCT Hackathon?

You can register on our official website by clicking the "Register Now" button. Fill out the registration form and create your account before the registration deadline.
